Visual Hierarchy

Pokémon

pokemon.com

The Pokémon homepage is an example of visual hierarchy because the text within the red region is divided into two parts. The first part of the text is bigger and white, the second block of text is smaller and a bit gray. Another example is that the first image is filling the width of the viewport, while the second image is not.

White Space and Clean Design

Apple

apple.com

The Apple website is the ultimate example for whitespace and clean design. The site uses whitespace around images of the phones convey a bold and clean display.

Hick's Law

Best Buy

bestbuy.com

Best Buy is a good example of Hick's Law, because the home page is chock-full of the categories of items it has for sale, thus driving the time of site use by users.
